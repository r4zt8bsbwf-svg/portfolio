About

Background

I'm a computer engineering graduate from Mississippi State University. My work sits at the intersection of hardware and software — embedded firmware, real-time systems, mechanical integration, and the messy details of getting all of it to cooperate in the physical world.

Outside of school, I spent five years at Chick-fil-A, most recently as a team leader, where I learned how operations, people, and systems actually run under pressure. That experience shapes how I approach engineering: reliability and clarity matter more than cleverness.

I'm currently looking for full-time roles in embedded systems, robotics, or systems engineering — ideally somewhere I can keep working across the hardware/software boundary.
